About Me

My Introduction

I am a Mechatronics Engineer pursuing a Bachelor of Mechatronics Engineering with Honors at Asia Pacific University (APU), driven by a strong curiosity for how mechanical systems, electronics, and software integrate to form intelligent, functional solutions. What motivates me most is transforming ideas into real-world systems that operate reliably and efficiently.

Throughout my academic journey, I have engaged in multidisciplinary projects involving sensor integration, embedded systems, and control logic. I enjoy hands-on experimentation, system debugging, and iterative improvement — the process of refining a system until theory becomes practical performance is where my passion for engineering truly shows.

I approach engineering with creativity, discipline, and a commitment to continuous learning. I aspire to contribute to professional environments where thoughtful design, technical precision, and responsibility are valued, and where engineering is not only about building systems, but about building them with purpose.
